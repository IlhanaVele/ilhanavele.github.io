International issues, theories, policies, and resolutions that interest me.

Growing up in a war torn Bosnia and Herzegovina has inspired me to engage in global politics by studying the field of International Relations. I am from Gornji Vakuf a small town in central Bosnia and Herzegovina. My hometown with the population of just about 19,000 is divided upon ethnic lines even today. The hatred that comes from this division could lead to another ethnic cleansing in the region. As my family and friends still reside in this environment I feel especially drawn to seek resolution for this crisis in the Balkans. Additionally, I love to travel and experience different cultures and political systems. Please read various papers that I wrote on these topics. Thank you for stopping by and please stay in touch.
